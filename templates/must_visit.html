Must Visit Spots in Acapulco

La Quebrada: A Legendary Dive into Adventure

La Quebrada is one of Acapulco’s most iconic attractions. Divers leap from heights of 40 feet (12 meters) and 80 feet (24 meters), timing their jumps to coincide with incoming waves. This precise calculation helps them avoid injury.

Often, the divers carry torches, adding a dramatic flair to their feats. Many of these divers are from families with a rich history of cliff diving. Although this tradition dates back many years, the La Quebrada Cliff Divers group was officially formed in 1934.

Witnessing these fearless divers against Acapulco’s stunning coastal backdrop is a thrilling and unforgettable experience.

Parque Papagayo: A Verdant Oasis in Acapulco

Parque Papagayo is a sprawling urban park located in the heart of Acapulco. This lush, green oasis spans over 52 acres (21 hectares) and offers a refreshing escape from the bustling city. Visitors can enjoy scenic walking paths, serene lakes, and beautifully manicured gardens, making it a perfect spot for relaxation and recreation.

The park is home to a variety of attractions, including playgrounds, a small zoo, and even an artificial lagoon. Families can spend the day exploring, with plenty of space for picnics, paddle boating, and taking in the natural beauty. Additionally, Parque Papagayo features numerous statues and monuments, adding a touch of cultural charm to its vibrant landscape.

Whether you're seeking a peaceful stroll or an adventure-filled day, Parque Papagayo offers something for everyone. Its combination of natural beauty and recreational activities makes it a must-visit destination in Acapulco.

Playa Icacos: A Coastal Paradise

Playa Icacos, one of Acapulco’s most popular beaches, is a haven for sun seekers and water sports enthusiasts. This expansive beach offers golden sands and crystal-clear waters, perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and a variety of water activities.

Visitors can enjoy jet skiing, parasailing, and beach volleyball, making it an ideal spot for both relaxation and adventure. The beach is also lined with numerous bars and restaurants, where you can savor local delicacies and refreshing drinks while enjoying the stunning coastal views.

Whether you’re looking to unwind under the sun or indulge in exciting beach activities, Playa Icacos provides a vibrant and welcoming atmosphere for all.

El Rollo Acapulco: Splashing Fun for Everyone

El Rollo Acapulco is a vibrant water park that offers endless fun for families and adventure seekers. With its thrilling water slides, wave pools, and lazy rivers, the park provides a perfect blend of excitement and relaxation.

The park features attractions for all ages, from gentle slides for the little ones to high-speed rides for the adrenaline junkies. Visitors can also enjoy a variety of pools and play areas, ensuring there’s something for everyone to enjoy.

Whether you’re looking to cool off on a hot day or seek adventure, El Rollo Acapulco is the ideal destination for a splashing good time.

Capilla de la Paz: A Haven of Serenity

Perched high on a hill, Capilla de la Paz (Chapel of Peace) offers breathtaking panoramic views of Acapulco Bay. This serene chapel, surrounded by lush gardens, is a popular spot for visitors seeking tranquility and stunning vistas.

The chapel’s iconic cross, visible from miles away, symbolizes peace and hope, making it a must-visit for those exploring the spiritual and scenic beauty of Acapulco. The peaceful environment and beautiful architecture provide a perfect retreat from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Whether you're looking to enjoy a moment of reflection or simply admire the spectacular views, Capilla de la Paz offers a unique and tranquil experience.
