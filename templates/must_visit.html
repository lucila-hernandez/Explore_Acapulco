Must Visit Spots in Acapulco

La Quebrada

Watch the famous cliff divers perform breathtaking dives from the cliffs of La Quebrada.

Fort of San Diego

Explore this historic fort and its museum to learn about Acapulco’s rich past.

Playa Icacos

Enjoy the sun and surf at one of Acapulco’s most popular beaches.

El Rollo Acapulco

A fun water park perfect for families and adventure seekers alike.

Capilla de la Paz (Chapel of Peace)

Visit this serene chapel offering stunning views of Acapulco Bay.
