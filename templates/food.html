Delicious food in Acapulco

Fine Dining

Mesón de los Ángeles

Mesón de los Ángeles is renowned for its exquisite Mexican and fusion cuisine, offering a luxurious dining experience. Guests are treated to a beautiful setting and gourmet dishes crafted with precision and care.

Sunset Lounge

Sunset Lounge specializes in Mexican and seafood dishes, providing not only delicious food but also a stunning view and exceptional service. It's the perfect spot for a romantic dinner or a special occasion.

Restaurante BellaVista

Restaurante BellaVista at Hotel Las Brisas Acapulco offers international cuisine with breathtaking views of the bay. The atmosphere is fantastic, and the dishes are crafted to perfection, making it a memorable dining experience.

Breakfast

La Finca Acapulco Costera

La Finca Acapulco Costera offers a delightful breakfast with excellent views of the bay. It's known for its fresh seafood and a cozy atmosphere, making it a great spot to start your day.

Mamma Gaia

Mamma Gaia is a comfortable and relaxing place that serves international and fusion breakfast dishes. It's a newer spot but has quickly become a favorite among locals and visitors alike.

La Cabaña de Caleta

La Cabaña de Caleta is a charming Mexican restaurant with a beautiful setting. It's known for its delicious breakfast dishes and a cozy atmosphere, making it a great place to start your day.

Snacks

Deli Frappe

Situated on Bernal Díaz del Castillo 26, Deli Frappe is known for its delicious sandwiches and coffee, making it a great spot for a quick and tasty snack

Mariscos Pipos

Located on Costera M. Alemán, opposite the Convention Center, Mariscos Pipos is famous for its seafood snacks, including shrimp cocktails and ceviche, perfect for a savory treat

La Concha Acapulco

It's a fantastic spot known for its delicious seafood snacks, including shrimp cocktails and ceviche. Located right on the beach, it offers a relaxing atmosphere with stunning views, making it a perfect place to enjoy some tasty snacks..
